Here are some of the most common questions that people have about fitness and working out.

Where is FitnessHub located?+

FitnessHub is located in Tesano, Accra, Ghana. Our gym is easily accessible and provides a welcoming environment for all fitness levels.

What are the operating hours of FitnessHub? +

We are open Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you offer personal training services?+

Yes, we offer one-on-one personal training sessions tailored to your fitness goals. Our certified trainers are here to help you achieve your best results.

How can I become a member of FitnessHub?+

To become a member, you can visit us at our location, or sign up online through our website. We offer flexible membership plans to suit your needs.

Do you offer group fitness classes?+

Yes, we offer a variety of group fitness classes, including yoga, Zumba, spinning, and strength training. Check our schedule for class timings and availability.

Is there a membership fee? +

We have flexible membership options starting from GHS 100/month.

Do you have beginner-friendly classes? +

Yes, we offer classes tailored for beginners to ease you into fitness.
